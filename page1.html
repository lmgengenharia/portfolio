Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental Engineer

Eng. Leonardo Guimarães

Leonardo de Mattos Guimarães was born in Joinville/SC - Brazil on 04/08/1988. Graduated in Environmental Engineering from UNIVALI - University of Vale do Itajaí in 2014, Postgraduate in Environmental Expertise and Audit from UNINTER - International University in 2016, Postgraduate in Occupational Safety Engineering from UNIFRAN - University of Franca in 2018, Graduate in Quality Engineering from FACJAR - Faculdades Jardins in 2020, and graduating in Civil Engineering from Faculdade Católica. Internal auditor of ISO 9001/2015, ISO 14001/2015, OHSAS 18001/2007, Lean Six Sigma Green Belt standards.
Began his professional experience in the duplication work of the BR-101 Highway on lot 27 in Torres/RS - Brazil in 2008, working as an intern in the Environmental Management sector at Construtora Queiroz Galvão, absorbing experiences of how works the execution of the environmental programs, and its application in each stage of the highway work. Still as an intern, he worked at the company LCG consultancy in Environmental Management, in Balneário Camboriú/SC - Brazil from 2012 to 2013, mainly accompanying waste management in the construction of large projects in Balneário Camboriú/SC - Brazil, in addition to other internal demands of the environmental consultancy.
In 2014, after graduating in Environmental Engineering, he was selected for the position of IMS Coordinator (Integrated Management System) at Construtora Queiroz Galvão, working on the New Guaíba's Bridge, a project with a R$650 million contract executed in Porto Alegre/RS - Brazil. On this occasion, he was responsible for the Quality, Environment, Health, Safety and Compliance sectors through the ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, ISO 37001/16 and ISO 19600/14 standards, and acting as an internal auditor of the aforementioned standards, ensuring the compliance with the requirements of the relevant rules and legislation. Also in the QHSE sectors (quality, occupational safety, environment and occupational health) acting as field manager, activities supervisor, responsible for training all employees regarding the IMS.
In 2019, he joined the energy sector / civil construction of transmission lines, acting as HSE Engineer at I.G. Transmission and Distribution, being the direct manager of several concurrent teams, around 50 employees, between higher, technical and operational levels.

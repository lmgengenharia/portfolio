Eng. Leonardo Guimarães

Leonardo de Mattos Guimarães was born in Joinville/SC - Brazil on 08/04/1988. His academic path is a degree in Environmental Engineering from UNIVALI - Universidade do Vale do Itajaí in 2014, Postgraduate in Expertise and Environmental Auditing from UNINTER - Universidade Internacional in 2016, postgraduate in Occupational Safety Engineering from UNIFRAN - Universidade de Franca in 2018, postgraduate in Quality Engineering from FACJAR - Faculdades Jardins in 2020, and graduate in Civil Engineering from Faculdade Católica in 2023. Internal auditor of ISO 9001/2015, ISO 14001/2015, OHSAS 18001/2007, and Lean Six Sigma Green Belt.
His professional experience began in the highway BR-101 duplication, in Torres/RS in 2008, working as an intern of Environmental Management sector at Construtora Queiroz Galvão, absorbing experiences of how the execution of the EBP - Evironmental Basic Plan works, as a result of the project's EIS - Environmental Impact Study, and its application at each stage of the road work. Still as an intern, he worked at the company LCG Consultoria em Gestão Ambiental, in Balneário Camboriú/SC from 2012 to 2013, mainly monitoring waste management in the construction of large projects in Balneário Camboriú/SC, in addition to other internal demands of the environmental consultancy.
In 2014, after graduating in Environmental Engineering, he was selected for the position of IMS (Integrated Management System) Coordinator at Construtora Queiroz Galvão, working on the New Guaíba's Bridge project. A project with a contract worth R$650 million, carried out in Porto Alegre/RS. On this occasion, he was responsible for the Quality, Environment, Occupational Safety and Compliance sectors through ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, ISO 37001/16 and ISO 19600/14, and acting as an internal auditor for the aforementioned standards, ensuring the compliance with the requirements of the relevant standards and legislation. Also in the QHSE sectors (quality, work safety, environment and occupational health) acting as field manager, activities supervisor, responsible for training all employees in the IMS demands.
In 2019, he joined the energy/civil construction sector of power transmission lines, acting as HSE manager at I.G. Transmission and Distribution, being the direct manager of several teams in parallel, around 50 employees, including graduate, technical and operational levels.
In 2020, he began his journey in entrepreneurship, opening a consulting company focused on the areas of Environmental Engineering, Occupational Safety Engineering and Quality Engineering, with a greater focus on the environmental sector, working with environmental licensing, various environmental authorizations, and environmental studies .
In 2022, still in the energy sector, he worked on the TL 230kV Laranjeiras - Gentio do Ouro II at the Assuruá 4 Wind Complex (Wind farms in Bahia), and on the TL 230kV Dianópolis II - Gurupi (Tocantins), for the company LTSUL Energia, coordinating the HSE team and the management and execution of the demands of the Occupational Safety, environment and occupational health sectors.
Currently works as a corporate coordinator of health and safety at Neoenergia, a multinational company in the energy sector with Transmission Lines and energy substation construction, operation and maintenance (O&M).
